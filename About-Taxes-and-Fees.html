Learn More About Taxes and Fees

Taxes, Surcharges and Fees

Sales Taxes and other fees shown online are calculated on the cost of the reservation plus any extras or protection products selected with the vehicle reservation that are subject to taxes or fees. Additional taxes and fees will be applied to any extras or protection products purchased at the rental counter.

Sales Taxes & Other Government Mandated Charges

States, counties, cities, and other governmental authorities may impose sales taxes, VAT, gross receipts taxes, fees, and other mandatory charges, such as arena or stadium fees, on customers of car rental companies or car rental transactions. These charges appear in the charges section of the Rental Agreement.

Non-Government Mandated Charges & Non-Mandatory Airport or Hotel Related Charges

At some locations, RRR SUV RENTAL has elected to impose 'recovery fees' to recover or recoup all or a portion of its expenses related to a type of overhead. At certain airport locations where RRR SUV RENTAL is an on-airport concessionaire, RRR SUV RENTAL has elected to impose a charge, often referred to as a Concession Fee Recoupment or Concession Fee Recovery, to recover all or a portion of the concession fees which it is obligated to pay to the airport.

Further, many airports impose a fee on revenue from off-airport rental car transactions at locations where RRR SUV RENTAL is an off-airport concessionaire. At these locations, RRR SUV RENTAL elects to impose a charge to recover all or a portion of this fee from customers. The fee, which varies by airport, will appear separately in the charges section of the Rental Agreement as Airport Fee, or the like.

Other recovery fees that RRR SUV RENTAL elects to impose at some locations include, without limitation:

A vehicle licensing and registration recovery fee, often called Vehicle Licensing Fee or 'VLF', which is the per day recovery of RRR SUV RENTAL 's average annual cost to license and register its fleet in the applicable jurisdiction. For rentals in New York, New York and Other Boroughs, such fee also includes recovery of costs imposed on RRR SUV RENTAL by the New York State Governmentbecause of renting vehicles in New York, New York and Other Boroughsthat are licensed and registered in New York State.
